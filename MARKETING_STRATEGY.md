# EketSupply Mobile App Marketing Strategy

**Tagline:** "Fix it Right, The First Time"

**Mission:** Connect Nigerian customers with verified skilled artisans for reliable, quality home services.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience

### Primary Customers
- **Urban homeowners** (25-55 years) in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt
- **Property managers** handling multiple units
- **Small business owners** needing maintenance services
- **Middle-income families** seeking reliable home repairs

### Primary Artisans
- **Experienced craftspeople** (3+ years) in plumbing, electrical, carpentry, painting
- **Small business owners** looking to expand customer base
- **Verified professionals** wanting to build digital reputation
- **Mobile-savvy artisans** (18-50 years) comfortable with smartphones

---

## 📊 Market Positioning

### Unique Value Propositions

**For Customers:**
- ✅ Verified artisans with background checks
- 📸 Portfolio galleries showing real work quality

- 🗺️ Location-based discovery (find nearby pros)

**For Artisans:**

## 🚀 Launch Strategy (Months 1-3)

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-2)

**Build Anticipation:**
1. **Create social media accounts**
   - Instagram: @eketsupply
   - Facebook: EketSupply Nigeria
   - Twitter/X: @eketsupply_ng
   - TikTok: @eketsupply

2. **Teaser campaign**
   - Post countdown graphics
   - Share "coming soon" videos
   - Highlight problem: "Tired of unreliable artisans?"
   - Tease solution: "Something amazing is coming..."

3. **Build email list**
   - Landing page with waitlist signup
   - Offer: "Early access + ₦2,000 first booking discount"
   - Target: 500+ signups before launch

4. **Partner outreach**
   - Contact 50 verified artisans for beta testing
   - Offer: Free verification + featured listing
   - Goal: 20 artisans ready at launch

### Phase 2: Soft Launch (Week 3)

**Limited Release:**
1. **Beta testing with waitlist**
   - Invite first 100 customers
   - Invite 20 verified artisans
   - Collect feedback and testimonials

2. **Referral incentives**
   - Customers: ₦1,000 credit per referral
   - Artisans: ₦5,000 per successful referral
   - Goal: Viral growth through word-of-mouth

3. **Local PR**
   - Press release to Nigerian tech blogs
   - Pitch story: "Nigerian startup solving artisan trust problem"
   - Target: TechCabal, Techpoint Africa, Nairametrics

### Phase 3: Public Launch (Week 4)

**Full Market Entry:**
1. **Launch event**
   - Virtual webinar for artisans
   - Demo app features live
   - Q&A session
   - Record and share on YouTube

2. **Promotional offers**
   - First 1,000 customers: 20% off first booking
   - First 100 artisans: Free verification
   - Limited time: Free emergency booking upgrade

3. **Influencer partnerships**
   - Micro-influencers (10K-50K followers)
   - Home improvement, DIY, property content creators
   - Budget: ₦50K-₦100K per post
   - Target: 5-10 influencers

## 💰 Optimized Marketing Budget (First 3 Months)

### Revised Budget - Focus on High-ROI Channels

| Category | Monthly | 3-Month Total | Expected ROI |
|----------|---------|---------------|-------------|
| Organic social media | ₦20,000 | ₦60,000 | 15:1 |
| Referral program incentives | ₦100,000 | ₦300,000 | 8:1 |
| Micro-influencers | ₦50,000 | ₦150,000 | 10:1 |
| WhatsApp marketing | ₦10,000 | ₦30,000 | 12:1 |
| Community partnerships | ₦30,000 | ₦90,000 | 9:1 |
| Targeted Facebook ads | ₦40,000 | ₦120,000 | 6:1 |
| Content creation | ₦30,000 | ₦90,000 | 7:1 |
| Offline (flyers, posters) | ₦20,000 | ₦60,000 | 5:1 |
| **Total** | **₦300,000** | **₦900,000** | **8:1** |

**Blended ROI Target:** 8:1 (₦8 revenue per ₦1 marketing spend)

### Why This Works Better for Nigerian Market

1. **Lower CAC:** Nigerian users respond better to word-of-mouth and community trust
2. **Higher LTV:** Average customer books 3-4 times/year at ₦15K-₦30K per booking
3. **Viral coefficient:** Referral program can achieve 1.5+ (each user brings 1.5 more)
4. **Commission model:** 15% platform fee means every ₦100K in bookings = ₦15K revenue

## 🚨 Crisis Management

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Bad review goes viral**
- Respond publicly within 2 hours
- Investigate and resolve privately
- Offer compensation if warranted
- Share resolution publicly

**Artisan misconduct**
- Immediate suspension pending investigation
- Customer refund + compensation
- Public statement on actions taken
- Review verification process

**App technical issues**
- Status page with updates
- Social media announcements
- Email notifications to affected users
- Compensation (credits) for inconvenience
