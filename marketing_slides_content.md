## Slide 14: Success metrics track acquisition, activation, retention, revenue, and referral performance

AARRR framework measures entire customer journey from first app install through viral referral loop. Weekly monitoring enables rapid optimization of underperforming channels while doubling down on highest-ROI tactics. Target 40% repeat booking rate by month three signals strong product-market fit.

**Acquisition Metrics:**
- App downloads by source (organic, paid, referral)
- Cost per install (CPI) by channel
- Website visitors and landing page conversion rate
- Social media follower growth and engagement rate
- Press mentions and earned media value

**Activation Metrics:**
- Account creation rate (% of downloads)
- Profile completion rate (full name, location, preferences)
- First search performed (% of signups)
- Artisan profile views (average per user)
- Time to first booking (days from signup)

**Retention Metrics:**
- 7-day, 30-day, 90-day retention rates
- Repeat booking rate by cohort
- Days between bookings (frequency)
- Churn rate and reasons for leaving
- Net Promoter Score (NPS) tracking

**Revenue Metrics:**
- Gross booking value (total transaction volume)
- Platform revenue (15% commission)
- Average booking value by category
- Service package adoption rate
- Emergency booking percentage (higher margin)

**Referral Metrics:**
- Viral coefficient (users brought per existing user)
- Referral conversion rate (% of invites that sign up)
- Referral booking rate (% of referred users who book)
- Top referrers and reward payouts
- Referral channel effectiveness (WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook)
