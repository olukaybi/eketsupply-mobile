- **Current:** 42% penetration = 94M smartphones

- **Data costs dropping:** ₦1K/GB → ₦200/GB (5x cheaper in 5 years)

### 3. Middle Class Growth

- **Paystack, Flutterwave:** 99.5% transaction success rates
