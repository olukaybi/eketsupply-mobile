## 1. Unit Economics Analysis

### Revenue Per Transaction

| Metric | Value | Calculation |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Average booking value | ₦20,000 | Industry average for skilled trades |
| Platform commission | 15% | Competitive with global marketplaces |
| Revenue per booking | **₦3,000** | ₦20,000 × 15% |

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

| Channel | CAC | Conversion Rate |
|---------|-----|-----------------|
| Referrals | ₦500 | 25% (viral growth) |
| WhatsApp | ₦800 | 18% (high intent) |
| Micro-influencers | ₦1,200 | 12% (trust transfer) |
| Organic social | ₦600 | 15% (content marketing) |
| **Blended CAC** | **₦850** | Weighted average |

### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

| Metric | Value | Rationale |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Bookings per year | 3.5 | Quarterly maintenance + emergencies |
| Revenue per customer/year | ₦10,500 | 3.5 × ₦3,000 |
| Customer lifespan | 3 years | Platform stickiness |
| **Total LTV** | **₦31,500** | ₦10,500 × 3 years |
| Retention rate | 65% | Year 2+ (network effects) |

### LTV:CAC Ratio

```
₦31,500 (LTV) ÷ ₦850 (CAC) = 37:1
```

**Industry Benchmark:** 3:1 is healthy, 5:1+ is excellent  
**EketSupply:** **37:1** — Exceptionally strong unit economics

## 5. Competitive Advantages (Moat)

### Network Effects
- **Two-sided marketplace:** More artisans attract more customers, and vice versa
- **Data moat:** Smart scheduling and matching improve with usage
- **Trust infrastructure:** Verification, badges, reviews create switching costs

### Technology Differentiation
- **Emergency booking:** 2-hour guarantee competitors can't match
- **Location intelligence:** Map-based discovery with response time data
- **Portfolio system:** Before/after photos + video testimonials

### Cost Advantages
- **WhatsApp-first:** 90%+ penetration, near-zero distribution cost
- **Viral referrals:** Built-in ₦5K/₦1K rewards drive 1.5+ viral coefficient
- **Community partnerships:** 60-70% lower CAC than paid ads
